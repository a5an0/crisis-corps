Story - Haiti and OSM

During the Haiti earthquake aftermath, users around the globe contributed their knowledge to the Open Street Maps project which helped to fill in knowledge gaps on maps of the effected area, enabling relief efforts to be better informed of their surroundings. Thousands of text messages were translated into languages that rescuers could understand, and lives were saved as a result. All of that work was done by individuals, sitting at their computer and volunteering their time and effort to make a difference.
